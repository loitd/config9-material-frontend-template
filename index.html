Company Bio

I am a Python/NodeJS Fullstack Developer searching for a good, modern, lightweight and fast frontend framework for my next and future projects. Unfortunately, I found that Google Material Design is what I need but I couldn't find any template suits my demand. I grabbed a Starter Template from Materializecss and modified it myown to adapt my needs. I call it CMFT (Config9 Material FrontEnd Template).
